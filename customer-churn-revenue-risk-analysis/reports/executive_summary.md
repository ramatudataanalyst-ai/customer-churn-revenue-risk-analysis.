# Executive Summary: Customer Churn Analysis

## Problem
Customer churn is negatively impacting recurring revenue and long-term growth.

## Key Findings
- Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn rates
- Long-term contracts significantly reduce churn
- A small segment of customers contributes disproportionately to revenue at risk

## Financial Impact
Churned customers represent a significant portion of monthly recurring revenue,
indicating a strong opportunity for targeted retention strategies.

## Recommendations
- Migrate high-risk customers to longer-term contracts
- Focus retention efforts on high-revenue segments
- Introduce bundled services to increase customer stickiness
